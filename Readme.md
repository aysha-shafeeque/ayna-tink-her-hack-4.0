### Project Description
[Planyx ‚Äì Smart Study Planner
A web app to organize your study schedule by subject, deadline, and difficulty. It generates a daily calendar with timers, tracks progress, supports dark mode, and lets you export your plan as a PDF.]

### The Problem statement
[Students often struggle to plan their study schedule efficiently, balance multiple subjects, and track progress toward deadlines. Existing methods like notebooks or simple apps lack automated time allocation, priority-based scheduling, and progress tracking, making it hard to stay organized and productive.]

### The Solution
[Planyx lets students add subjects with deadlines and difficulty levels, then automatically generates a daily calendar with time allocation, interactive timers, progress tracking, and PDF export for easy planning and review.]
